Tax Representation

As your Tax representative, we act on your behalf during interactions with the IRS, especially during audits or disputes. We help prepare necessary documents, develop strategies, and communicate with the IRS to protect your rights and interests.

As a Licensed Enrolled Agent with unlimited practice rights granted by the Internal Revenue Service, we are obligated to discharge our duties in your best interest and in the most professionally acceptable manner.

Tax Preparation

We provide top-notch tax preparation services, completing and filing tax returns for individuals and small and mid-size businesses. You can be assured of high-quality professional service delivery.

Tax Issues Resolution

We also offer Tax Issues Resolution services, like audits, penalties, or unpaid taxes. We deploy mastery in ensuring the best resolution options are reached on your behalf. Available options include payment plans, offers in compromise, and professional negotiations with the IRS on your behalf.

Tax Planning

As your preferred Tax Pros, we analyze your financial situation aimed at minimizing your tax liability and maximizing the use of legally available deductions, credits, and exemptions. We also support your strategic decision-making process throughout the year to ensure compliance with tax laws while reducing your adverse tax exposure.

We go the extra mile by benchmarking your before and after results so you can quantify the value-added impact of our service.

Bookkeeping and Accounting

We help you in the process of recording daily financial transactions and maintaining accurate financial records. We also support you in analyzing and summarizing this financial data to prepare reports and provide strategic insights for decision-making.
